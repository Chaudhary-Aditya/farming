Benefits of Organic Farming

Organic farming offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Improved soil health and fertility
  • Higher nutritional value of crops
  • Reduced chemical exposure for farmers and consumers
  • Enhanced biodiversity and ecosystem health
  • Support for local economies and communities

Common Organic Farming Practices

Some common practices include:

  • Crop rotation to maintain soil fertility
  • Composting to enrich soil
  • Using natural pest control methods
  • Planting cover crops to prevent erosion
  • Maintaining a diverse range of crops
